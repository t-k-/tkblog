Definition of "match"



  • Partial: $a \color{red}{x(1}+b)$
  • Sub-expression: $ax(\color{red}{1+b})$
  • Wildcard matching:
    • Is $\ a\color{red}{?} \ $ and $\ a \color{red}{+b} \ $ good?
    • Is $\ a\color{red}{?} \ $ and $\ a \color{red}{+b} \ $ good?
